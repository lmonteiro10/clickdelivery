About us

Started in 2016, Click Delivery has the Know-how to support restaurants, bars and all those who provide delivery option to clients. We have highly trained professionals in the area of ​​support, consultancy, analysis and development, offering quality services and agile solutions, providing customers with a better service, since in addition to having a comprehensive system, we are always adapting to the needs of the customer, developing new tools and forms of management through customizations. Founded in Vila Velha - Espírito Santo and today with thousands of customers across Brazil, Click Delivery seeks daily to exceed expectations and offer a new and extraordinay to customers and employees. For these reasons, the progressive increase in customers, as well as the permanence of the current ones, shows us that we are increasingly providing a quality service, both in relation to the product offered as well as in the support provided.
